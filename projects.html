    Fossil-GIS

    An analysis of the clustering of Canadian Fossil Data

    Started: Oct 2020, Finished: Dec 2020
    Used: dash, numpy, geopandas

    Implemeneted and analysed the following algorithms: Graham Scan, k-means, CLARANS, BIRCH, Agglomerative Hierarchical Clustering: Average, and Minimum Linkages, and DBScan

    The algorithms implemented were simple and fast enough that they could be interactively, allowing the user to experiment with different parameters

    cuACS: Animal Shelter Application

    CUACS - Carleton University Animal Care Service
    Team Solo, COMP3004 w19
    Companion Page to school project, no code, solutions, or technical stuff mentioned, as per profs requirements.

    The cuACS system was designed with the purpose of matching animals with clients that meet their needs, and matching clients with pets that fit their preferences.

    How to get access to the system:

    If you are a new client, apply to join at your local animal shelter.

    If you are a new staff, get a login from your supervisor


    The Staff Dashboard:


    • To view client profiles click profiles on the sidebar or the Go button under View Client Profiles
    • To view, edit, and add new animals to the system click the pets button on the sidebar, or the Go button under View and Edit Animal Profiles
    • To run the matching algorithm and get the result click the Match button on the sidebar or the Go button under Match Clients with Animals
    • To logout of the system click the logout button in the lower left hand corner
    • To close the window click the X button in the right upper hand corner
    • To view this help page at any time click a question mark button

    View all Registered Pets:

    • To view a pets profile click on the row with its name
    • To add a new pet click on the Add button at the buttom of the window

    Add new pets to the System:

    • To add a new pet to the system fill out the form with the animal's information
    • To select an image from your filesystem click Browse and select an image, to use default image do nothing.
    • To submit the new pet to the system click the submit button
    • To cancel the new entry click cancel
